King Bob

King Bob is the ruler of the land and for years he has ruled with strength and wisdom. While he has flourished as a leader, he knows his time as the King is slowly coming to an end. Having no heirs of his own, he has set out a challenge to find his successor. The lords of the land are challenged to get artifacts from each of the other lands showing their strength. while doing so they must use wisdom to know what to do with their winning after collecting all the artifacts they will then challenge King Bob for his thrown.
