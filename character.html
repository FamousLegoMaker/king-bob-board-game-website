Meryk the Panda Bear

The panda tribe is celebrating a small victory after obtaining the katana belonging to the Asian black bears. The celebration is short-lived as recent news of the sun bear tribe encroaching on the camp has reached their leader. The cunning nature of the invasion has heightened the senses of the panda tribe as they await retaliation on top of this new threat.

Smokey the Grizzly Bear

A bear that is descended from two separate clans. He spends the day primarily foraging for food. He scours the land searching for the best food. Trained in using an ax has helped him hone the skill of procuring caches left high up in the trees. Honing this skill has made him a leader in the grizzly community. It has also been proved to be an effective negotiating tool when other bears seek to topple his reign.

Arrluk the Polar Bear

The Polar Bear remains at odds with the Grizzly bear over the territory dispute with National Parks. The Grizzly Bear clan has enjoyed reigning the region but soon the days are numbered as conflict is occurring in its ranks. The Polar Bear is taking advantage of the recent blizzards to advance and sneak into the opposing territory. All it needs to do now is lie in wait.

Benjamin the Black Bear

Being harassed by the sugar tax and privateers imposing on his moonshine business has fueled the fighting spirit. He has kept his powder dry and flint ready for anyone that seeks to destroy his way of life. Benjamin Ursus is ready to take the fight to the British or to make his last stand. He will not let the Tory neighbors ruin 100s of years of tradition by running down his mountain home.

Wilford the Sloth Bear

A parent suffering the loss of their cubs to poachers has reignited the fighting spirit within them. They keep their kukri close during travel and even closer when raiding termite mounds. They are on the hunt for the ones responsible for the death of their young cubs and maiming their mate. They keep to themselves to ensure that they are alert and aware of their surroundings.

Jukumari the Spectacled Bear

Jukumari, focused on staying in the trees hunting for food, keeps to himself as most predators stay closer to the ground. He is able to hunt with his bolo but opts for the gentle vegetation of the native land. He is keenly aware of all the flora in the forest but has noticed changes in his neighbors and wonders if the peaceful life he once knew is about to change.
