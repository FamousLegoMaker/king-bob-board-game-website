Objective

Collect all artifacts while defending your own, and reach King Bob to be the next heir to the throne.

Battle

Use all your resources to attack and defend artifacts. Player with the most War Points win and can collect or defend their artifacts.

Ending

Once you have collected all artifacts, you and two warriors will reach King Bob's throne. If you are able to defend King Bob, you will be the next heir to the throne.
